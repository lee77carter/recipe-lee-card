Crackin Salad

Prep Time: 10 Min

Cook Time: 10 Min

Total Time: 20 Min

This delicious green based salad is crackin and provides equal energy to a cup of coffee. sure to love!

Ingredients

  • 1 1/2 bag of kale
  • 1 1/2 bag of mixed greens or spinach
  • 1 1/2 bag of ice lettuce
  • Crumbled blue cheese
  • 1/4 teaspoon baking soda
  • Packaged egg white bites
  • 1 cherry tomatoes
  • 1 blueberries
  • 1 cup strawberries
  • 1/2 cup of kimchi

Directions

  1. Airfry egg white bites for 20 minutes.
  2. Mix all veggies and fruits in a bowl and stir.
  3. Sprinkle blue cheese crumbles throughout.
  4. Add dressing of choice.
  5. Add kimchi on to the side.

Savory Potatoes

Prep Time: 15 Min

Cook Time: None

Total Time: 15 Min

Tender and flavorful chopped potatoes, generously adorned with an array of savory toppings typically associated with baked potatoes, create a mouthwatering and satisfying culinary delight.

Ingredients

  • 1lb of potatoes
  • 1 cup of sharp cheese
  • 1/2 cup of scallions
  • 1 tablespoon of blue cheese dressing
  • 1 teaspoon salt
  • 1 teaspoon of oat butter

Directions

  1. Clean, boil, and cut potatoes in cubes.
  2. Add oat butter, and salt.
  3. Add sharp cheese, and scallions.
  4. Pour blue cheese over the top.
